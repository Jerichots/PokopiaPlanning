# Landscape & Building Plan

Master city planning guide for all areas. Houses are max 10x10 blocks and must contain at least 3 unique furniture items.

### Core Design Pillars
1. **Water access in every area** — Ponds, rivers, fountains, waterfalls, or ocean. Use Stockpile Water, fresh water, and mini floodgates to control flow.
2. **Urban districts** — Every area gets a dedicated commercial/civic zone with shops (Poke Mart, cash registers), Pokemon Center, fountains, and community spaces.
3. **Fighting courts / activity areas** — Punching bags, punching games, small stages for battles.
4. **No slum blocks** — Houses spaced with gardens, parks, and sightlines. Stagger placement, vary sizes.

## General Design Principles

### Spacing & Aesthetics
- **Never pack houses wall-to-wall.** Minimum 3 blocks between houses, 5+ preferred.
- **Every house gets a yard/garden.** Even 2 blocks of flowers or hedges transforms the look.
- **Roads should be 2-3 blocks wide.** Single-block paths feel cramped; 4+ feels wasteful.
- **Alternate house sizes.** Mix dens (small), huts, cottages, and houses to avoid monotony.
- **Stagger placement.** Don't line houses up in a perfect grid — offset every other row.

### Vertical Building (127 blocks max height)
- **Rocky Ridges**: Tiered cave districts carved at different elevations, mine shafts descending from peaks, lava falls cascading down cliff faces
- **Sparkling Skylands**: Skyscrapers (department store, steel tower), multi-level districts connected by elevator platforms
- **Bleak Beach**: Lighthouse tower, harbor cranes, elevated boardwalk sections
- **Palette Town**: Tall showcase builds, Altar of Flame spire, Freezing Chambers peaks
- Use **stone/wooden/iron steps** for accessible elevation changes
- **Elevator platforms** and **lift platforms** for multi-story buildings
- **Iron scaffold** as exterior fire escapes / industrial accents
- **Protruding walls** create balconies and overhangs
- **Ladders** (wooden/iron) for vertical cave access in Rocky Ridges

|------|---------|--------|
| Withered Wastelands | Mushroom streetlight, mushroom lamp | Glowing mushrooms, slender candles |
| Bleak Beach | Harbor streetlight, shell lamp | Lanterns, string lights |
| Rocky Ridges | Town streetlight, wall torch | Eerie candles, crystal clusters, glowing stone |
| Sparkling Skylands | Double streetlight, chic streetlight | Neon flooring, cube lights, spotlight |
| Palette Town | Garden light, plain lamp | String lights, surface lights |
